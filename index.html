Micro-Scale Printing Services

01

Two-Photon Polymerization (2PP)

Sub-micron feature printing via femtosecond laser two-photon polymerization. 200nm minimum feature for photonic waveguides, micro-optics, and nano-scale MEMS. IP-Dip and IP-S resins for biocompatible and optical applications.

02

Nano-DLP Micro-Printing

High-throughput micro-scale production via nano-DLP. 2µm layer thickness for microfluidic chip production, micro-lens arrays, and MEMS prototypes. 10× faster than 2PP for features above 5µm.

03

Microfluidics & Lab-on-Chip

Custom microfluidic devices with channel widths from 50µm. Multi-layer channel networks, valve seats, and integrated electrode features in a single print. Biocompatible materials for cell culture and diagnostic applications.

MICRO-SCALE MATERIALS

Microscale Printing Materials

Microscale printing demands specialized resins with controlled refractive index, biocompatibility, and mechanical stability at sub-100µm feature scales.

  • ● IP-Dip (2PP) — general purpose nano-scale resin
  • ● IP-S (2PP) — enhanced sensitivity for large nano-structures
  • ● OrmoComp — biocompatible optical-grade micro-resin
  • ● PEGDA hydrogel — cell-compatible microfluidic channels
  • ● Ceramic-loaded resin for micro-scale ceramics
  • ● Conductive polymer for micro-electrode integration
